About

Welcome to Lucky Mirlo's musical dimension! Where you will find immersed guitars, a juicy groove and deep soundscapes. At the age of 13, Lucky Mirlo made his first close-up to the music in Barcelona-Venezuela. Being influenced by bands like Guns N' Roses, Metallica, Avenged Sevenfold, and Gilberto Santa Rosa (yeah... really). At that time he made his first band, but he knew that wasn't the place where rockstars reach the top, but the reggaetoneros. So he tried his luck in another country In 2015 he was the guitarist and second voice of "Opposite States" a band from Florida, USA. But the circumstances made him move to Santiago Chile. However, there was the same case as Venezuela (make reggaeton or die). So, he finally decide to move to Europa, creating a solid solo project". Now in Germany, he became a part of the band "Rinoville" for a few months, until he got trapped in his home by the pandemic and started working on a solo project influenced by the flow of Jazz, Hip-Hop, R&B and always some fresh guitars. This is how the EP “CANCELLED MIA DATE” came. So here it is. Reach Lucky Mirlo on Social Media and stream CANCELLED MIA DATE, which is currently available on al the music streaming services. (I suggest you do it right know... before he start to make reggaeton and blow it up).
